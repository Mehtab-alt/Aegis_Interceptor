Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a vulnerability within AEGIS Interceptor, please follow our 3-step Coordinated Vulnerability Disclosure (CVD) process:

Private Contact: Email the maintainer with the subject "VULNERABILITY REPORT - AEGIS". dispositial@gmail.com
Validation: Maintainers will acknowledge within 72 hours. Please do not share details publicly during this phase.
Coordinated Release: Once a patch is developed, we will release an update and provide credit to the researcher.
